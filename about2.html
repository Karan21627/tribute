About

I am an aspiring and dedicated individual with a passion for creativity and a strong drive to make a meaningful impact. Over the years, I have developed a diverse skill set and a unique perspective that I bring to everything I do.

My journey began with a deep-seated love for the arts. I am a visual artist, specializing in painting and digital illustration. My work often explores themes of identity, culture, and the human experience. Through my art, I aim to provoke thought and evoke emotions, sparking meaningful conversations about the world we live in.

In addition to my artistic endeavors, I am a lifelong learner and an avid problem solver. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, which has equipped me with a strong foundation in programming, data analysis, and problem-solving. I thrive in challenging environments and enjoy tackling complex issues head-on.
